## Core Strategy
A high-intent affiliate review, hobby curation, and project resource platform targeting heavy-duty leather crafting and specialized gear enthusiasts.

## Crossover Target Audience

### 1. The Heavy-Duty Gear Enthusiast
- **Profile:** Knife guys, hunters, and metal detectorists
- **Needs:** Rugged field solutions (cross-draw sheaths, multi-welt survival knife casings, Lesche & Garrett combo tool rigs)
- **Intent:** Purchase ready-made solutions or learn to build custom gear

### 2. The Aspiring Leatherworker
- **Profile:** Complete beginners or hobbyists
- **Needs:** Exact, vetted tools, supplies, and waterproofing materials to build or maintain field gear
- **Intent:** Learn craftsmanship through guided projects and curated tool recommendations
